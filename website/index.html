About us

The house behind the jersey

Jaypee Sports began with a simple, real problem. Quality sportswear was hard to find — and what little existed wasn't built for regular use. For our founders, sportspersons themselves, that was a daily challenge. So they started making it themselves.

Teammates noticed the difference — in comfort, in durability, in how the clothing held up — and word spread as players carried it to matches across states. From just four sewing tables, the operation grew into a dedicated manufacturing unit built to meet demand without compromising quality.

Today we're trusted by schools, teams and institutions across the state — not just for supply, but for consistency. Our products are known to last: used over years, not seasons. That promise has a name — Quality Par Excellence.
