About us

The house behind the jersey

Jaypee Sports is a sportswear and sports equipment house from Ranchi, serving athletes, schools, colleges, clubs and institutions across India. From match-day uniforms to training gear, everything we make carries one promise — Quality Par Excellence.

We design, manufacture and deliver complete kits under one roof: apparel for every sport, equipment for every game, and custom teamwear built around each team's identity.
